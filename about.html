About this project

This site was built for IT 3203 — Web Development across three milestones: a hand-written HTML/CSS site, a JavaScript self-assessment quiz, and finally a fully responsive, mobile-friendly redesign with finalized research content.

The research content presents a technical analysis of the Tor network: its origins as a U.S. Naval Research Laboratory project, how its three-hop onion-routing circuits and layered encryption work, and the dual-use role Tor plays for both privacy advocates and security analysts today.

This site is a class project for IT 3203 Web Development.
