Research on Web Evolution: A Technical Analysis of the Tor Network

The World Wide Web was designed for open, transparent access to information - not for network-layer privacy. Every request you send exposes metadata that reveals who is talking to whom. This site explores Tor, the most widely deployed low-latency anonymity network, from its origins in naval research to the cryptography that keeps millions of users untraceable today.
